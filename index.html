What Are We?

D&S Dashtrans Ltd is a Somerset based, husband and wife team, offering van & man light goods haulage, specialising in Hot Shot loads. Need your goods delivered fast? We collect from you and drive your goods directly to their destination anywhere in the UK and Europe. 20 years experience in the haulage business makes us a reliable, affordable choice for your delivery needs. We run an XLWB Crafter Panel van which can transport up to 5 euro pallets, 4 standard pallets to a maximum weight of 1000kg.
