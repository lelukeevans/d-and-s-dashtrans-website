About Us

D&S Dashtrans Ltd is founded on old fashioned principles aided by modern technology. You will always get to speak to Dave or Sheila, on the phone, emails or text, whichever suits you best. We pride ourselves on professionalism, courtesy, respect and approachability.

"Hi, I'm Dave Evans, Director and Driver of Dashtrans. I spent around 20 years driving HGV class 1 in the UK and Europe while supporting my young family. In 2014 my wife and I decided to open our own business making good use of the experience gained over the 20 years of trucking and tramping. I love to drive, see new places and meet with people, old friends and new. I treat you and your customers how I like to be treated, with respect and a smile."

"Hi, I'm Sheila Evans, the other half and company secretary of Dashtrans. Before having children I worked in the accounts department of farming and quarrying. I took a long employment break to raise my family while Dave was driving long hours in the truck. If you have a query with your account, it's me you'll speak to, not an automated system. If you get the answer phone I'm probably walking the dogs! Just remember to leave a phone number and I'll call you straight back."
